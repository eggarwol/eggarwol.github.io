Projects

• Bored.io

Bored.io is my most recent project. The idea is, if you're bored and have no idea what you want to do, you'll end up just mindlessly browsing your phone trying to do something. This app allows you to input your basic interests and how much time and money you can spend on an activity, and from that it will give you a suggestion as to what you should do in your free time. It can show you new sports to play and where to play them, or what movies are currently in theaters and where to get tickets. Eventually the plan is to try and have some sort of social interaction in the app where users can connect with friends or other users that also share the same interests.

• DigiDash

DigiDash is an in-car digital dashboard that gives the user information about their car that they wouldn't normally have access to. This project gives the user the ability to create digital gauges and fully customize a home screen of those gauges. This project is an open source work in progress that was created and designed for my Software Engineering Concepts class at the University at Buffalo. Detailed info and how to setup DigiDash can be found here.

• 3D Printing

For the past 3 years I've tinkered in the world of 3D printing. For the DigiDash project above, we needed a case for our Raspberry Pi and touchscreen. In our lab we had a Makerbot, but we lacked the filament necessary to print out multiple cases. I did my own research and found out that I could get an easily modifiable printer for about 300 dollars and my hobby sprung up from there. I've even been learning how to 3D model as well.
